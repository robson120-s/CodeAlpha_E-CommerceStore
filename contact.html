Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

We process orders within 1-2 business days. Domestic shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while international shipping varies by destination.

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Vintage and final sale items are non-returnable.

How do I know an item's condition?

Each product page includes detailed condition notes and photos of any flaws. We grade items from "Like New" to "Well-Loved" with complete transparency.

Do you buy used clothing?

Yes! We purchase quality secondhand items in good condition. Email us photos and details of what you'd like to sell for a quote.
